## About Me

I'm a **senior mobile app developer** and **AI engineer** who ships end‑to‑end: from the first wireframe to the App Store release and the AI feature that keeps users coming back. I work as a freelance/contract engineer and founder of [**Flowdesk**](https://github.com/Flowdesktech), a remote AI‑first development studio.

- 📱 **Mobile specialist** — Native iOS (Swift, SwiftUI) and Android (Kotlin, Jetpack Compose), plus hybrid apps with React Native, Expo, and Flutter. Real App Store and Google Play release experience.
- 🤖 **AI engineer** — LLM orchestration, autonomous agents, RAG, evals, and automation pipelines using OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, LangChain, and open‑source models.
- 🌐 **Full‑stack web** — React, Next.js, TypeScript, Node.js, Python, Firebase, and Postgres. I ship the backend when the product needs it.
- 🔁 **Automation & pipelines** — Event‑driven workflows, integrations, CI/CD, and developer tooling.
- 🌍 **100% remote** — I work with teams and clients across time zones, async‑first.
- 🧑‍💻 **Open source** — I maintain several OSS projects under the [Flowdesk](https://github.com/Flowdesktech) organization.
